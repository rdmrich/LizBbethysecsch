Our story

The school is on land that measures 640 acres (1.00 sq mi), in Mukono District in the Central Region of Uganda. The main campus is approximately 25 kilometres , by road, east of Kampala, Uganda's capital and largest city.

The institution started in 2007 as a primary school for boys and girls in Uganda, East Africa. At that time it was called Liz bbethy Primary School. Later, the name changed to Liz bbethy secondary School
