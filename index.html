Our mission describes our school's purpose. Our vision depicts our desired future.The principles needed to achieve our mission and vision are articulated in our values. Finally, the actions we need to demonstrate to make our mission, vision and values a reality are described in our collective commitments. We have developed these statements in collaboration with our staff, students and families. They are part of our School Success Plan.

Our Head Teacher

Welcome to Liz Bbethy Secondary School’s website; a window to our vibrant school life. Dr. Liz once said, “Change is the law of life. And those who look only to the past or present are certain to miss the future”. The educational landscape has been evolving both nationally and globally the past decade and more so in recent years. Critical, creative and collaborative skills, global-mindedness, cultural otherness, discernment are among some of the important competencies we need to develop in our young. We need to respond to the fast changing world so that we will continue to remain relevant. We understand the need to bring what we teach and how we teach into this new era. At Liz Bbethy Secondary School, we have always been passionate about seeking out opportunities for intellectual and personal growth of our students in and beyond the classroom. We have made great strides over the years with our robust academic programme and strong character development pursuits. We believe in being the spark that lights the fire of an exciting pursuit of knowledge and growth.

Bbetty Nampijja
